Software Project Prerequisites - Part 1

Whenever I started a new project, sometimes on a new platform, I tried to make a list of prerequisites that are necessary for ensuring the project's success. I will try to group in this first post of the series what I believe is essential for being able to focus on development and not on side tasks.

First and most important is the source control. Be it SVN, Git, Hg, TFS or any other, it is essential that a software project is stored in a source control environment.

Secondly comes the Continuous Integration environment which is sometimes in close relationship with the Continuous Deployment environment, only because it is often executed by the same tool. For this I believe TeamCity and Jenkins are the most popular platforms.
